# Introduction-to--cybersecurity

## What is Cybersecurity?
Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting sensitive data from unauthorized access and malicious digital attacks.

## Why is Cybersecurity Important?
It is important because it protects companies from financial loss, operational disruption, and privacy violations.

## Three Examples
-Online banking
-Smartphones
-Hospitals
